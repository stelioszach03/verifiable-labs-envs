## What I'm interested in hearing about

- **Research collaborations** on conformal RL or scientific reasoning
  benchmarks.
- **Real-world capability evaluations** where the platform's
  compliance report would be a useful exhibit.
- **Custom envs** for domains we don't yet cover (especially
  bioinformatics, materials, geophysics).
- **Frontier-model providers** wanting to run the benchmark
  pre-release.

## What I'm *not* doing right now

- Production support contracts. v0.1 is alpha; reach out about a
  pilot but don't put production traffic on the public endpoint
  until v0.2.
- General consulting unrelated to the platform.
- Paid integrations for closed-source benchmarks. The platform is
  open; if your benchmark needs to stay private, the platform is the
  wrong fit.
