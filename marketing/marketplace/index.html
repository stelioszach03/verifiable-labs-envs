The marketplace for verifiable RL environments.

Verifiable Labs is building the substrate for trustworthy scientific reasoning: conformal-calibrated rewards, procedural problem regeneration, and a transparent forward operator behind every score. The marketplace is how we'll let the community contribute environments and how customers will evaluate frontier models on the tasks that matter to them.

Get notified at launch Read the code

Today: 10 environments, 5 frontier models benchmarked, hosted API, Python SDK. Tomorrow: a marketplace for community-contributed envs.

Why a marketplace

Standard ML benchmarks live on the public web. Frontier-model providers ingest them during training. The signal degrades.

Verifiable Labs solves this with procedural regeneration — every test instance is generated from a seed, never from a stored file — and conformal calibration — every reward includes a coverage-calibrated uncertainty term that's measurable, not vibes. The marketplace extends this to a community model: domain experts contribute environments for their field, the platform verifies regeneration + calibration, and customers pay for compute and aggregated leaderboard access.

Three roles

contributors

Researchers + domain experts

Ship a new environment for your field — inverse seismology, protein structure, materials, anything with a verifiable forward operator. Our scaffold + validator handles the boilerplate; you focus on the physics. Revenue share when models are evaluated on your env.

today: scaffold + validator shipped · payouts: planned for v0.3

consumers

Frontier-model providers

Evaluate your model on a benchmark you can't memorise. Get per-environment capability scores, calibration audits, and a compliance-grade PDF report ready for internal review. The API is open today; quotas + paid plans land in v0.2.

today: hosted API + SDK + leaderboard · billing: planned for v0.2

enterprise

Compliance + audit teams

Empirical capability evidence for AI-governance frameworks (NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act, ISO 42001). The platform itself asserts no legal compliance; it produces measurable, reproducible evidence your counsel can attach to their attestations.

today: compliance report template + PDF generator
